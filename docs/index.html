About

I am a graduate student pursuing Master of Science (Thesis) in Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University. My research focuses on heterogeneous CPU-GPU training for large language models, distributed systems, and high-performance computing. I have experience in machine learning systems, computer architecture, and edge computing. Previously, I worked as a Project Associate at the Middleware and Runtime Systems Lab at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), where I developed frameworks for efficient LLM training on heterogeneous systems. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from PES University.
